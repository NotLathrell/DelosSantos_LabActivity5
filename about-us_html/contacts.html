The Developer, Lathrell Delos Santos, is a 1st Year Bachelor of Science in Information Technology student at Gordon College. In his free time, he mostly spend some of it playing Valorant with his friends. He goes by the Nishimiya, which came from an anime movie entitled "The Silent Voice". In the game of Valorant, there are four classes that agents can be classified with, the Duelist, Sentinel, Initiator, and Controller. Duelists are self-sufficient fraggers who their team expects, through abilities and skills, to get high frags and seek out engagements first.Sentinels are defensive experts who can lock down areas and watch flanks, both on attacker and defender rounds.Initiators challenge angles by setting up their team to enter contested ground and push defenders away. Controllers are experts in slicing up dangerous territory to set their team up for success.
