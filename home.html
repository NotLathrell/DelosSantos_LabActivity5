VALORANT

Valorant is a popular team-based multiplayer first-person shooter (FPS) game developed and published by Riot Games. The game was first released in June 2020 and is available for Microsoft Windows. In Valorant, two teams of five players each battle it out in a series of rounds, with one team playing as the attacking side and the other as the defending side. Each player chooses a unique "Agent" character, each with their own set of abilities, and works with their team to complete objectives and eliminate the opposing team. The game is known for its fast-paced, strategic gameplay and high skill ceiling.
